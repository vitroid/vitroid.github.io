#Datatypes
## @AR3A - Atomic configuration format
|Data|Type|Description
|-----|-----|-----
|@AR3A|5 fixed characters|A tag
|N|integer|Number of atoms
|-----|-----|-----
|x y z|A triplet of real numbers separated by a space character.|Coordinates (in Angstrom).
|...|(repeat N times)|
|-----|-----|-----
